Privacy Policy for FastCal

Last updated: January 26, 2026

FastCal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by FastCal.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you use FastCal, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, age, gender, height, and weight to calculate your caloric needs.

Health Data: We access your step count and activity data via Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect only with your explicit permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the core functionality of the app: tracking your calories, fasting windows, and fitness progress. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Deletion

You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by going to Settings > Delete Account within the app or by contacting us at team.fastcal@gmail.com.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at team.fastcal@gmail.com.
